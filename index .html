Pooja Puri - Principal

I believe that purpose of Education is to open the minds to awareness, knowledge and expanding the horizons. I, as educationist am of the conviction that giving roots of values is significant so that our students remain firmly rooted to the ground. We give them wings of ambition, so that they can soar like falcons to touch the skies and endeavour to make them global citizens who can look beyond geographical boundaries and promote a culture of peace and universal brotherhood. Children acquire skills and knowledge easily if we can make the surroundings stimulating and purposeful. The philosophy of education, has always been meeting the needs of the individual child.
